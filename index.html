Features

Editing Suite

You'll find everything you need, and nothing you don't. No tables, no images or columns or complicated layouts. Just text.

It was good enough for Hemingway, Austen, and all the other greats. By God it's good enough for us, too.

With a single click, you can export your masterpiece to ePub, Kindle, PDF, or Word.

Collaboration

Invite editors to revise your work, and trusted readers to provide feedback prior to publication.

Version Control
